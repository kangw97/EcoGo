More than a Trip Planner

At EcoGo, our goal is to become the most environmentally progressive trip planner on the planet. We don’t want to just reduce the negative impact of pollution and plastic waste, we want to use EcoGo as a vehicle for change. We are the city’s number one eco-travel planner that provides eco-friendly travel destinations and activities for locals and tourists. Our purpose is to foster a healthy culture of walking, cycling, and social interaction.

WHAT WE OFFER:

Eco-friendly activities that pertain to a healthy lifestyle.
Access to ShawMobi bike locations and local bike rental shops.
Restaurants that are engaged in protecting the environment such as using sustainable materials and homegrown ingredients.
Supermarkets that have a commitment to sustainable adriculture and support local vendors and farmers.
Eco-friendly shops and brands that you never knew existed!
Automated guidance to each location.
